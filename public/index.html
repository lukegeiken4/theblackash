Food

At The Black Ash, we have some of the tastiest food that ever has come from a campfire. These are no hotdogs on a stick, but award winning platters cooked over an open fire. The New Jersey coast may not be the best place for outdoor wilderness camping, but we make sure that aren't missing out on the delicious food that comes from such activity.

Culture

Our founding team was made up of individuals with many different backgrounds all around the United States. They knew about the different assortments of campfire food from up north and Monatana campfire meat to down south where the desert of Arizona brings out some of the best Mexican soup dishes around. We bring the food and aesthetics right to your door without the hastle of setting up for the night. Come on by and peek inside the camping world we have brought to you.
